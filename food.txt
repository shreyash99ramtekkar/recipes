Omlet
Step 1: chop some onion and chillies 
Step 2: Break 2-3 eggs into one bowl
Step 3: Add the chopped onion and chillies in the eggs bowl
Step 4: Add the pepper and salt into the eggs bowl
Step 5: Mix all the thing well.
Step 6: Put the mixture in medium high pan.
Step 7: Wait until it get cook form one side.
Step 8: flip it so that other side also get cooked
Step 9: Serve it hot in the plate.
